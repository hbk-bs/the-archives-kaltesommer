The p5.js library introduced us to new perspectives on utilizing code in an artistic way by enabling the programmer to define and control every visible element. For example, in the perspective sketch 'Size,' the attributes are coded for each circle, rectangle, or other shape, allowing elements to be combined into a cohesive composition. The primary challenge was ensuring that the sketches effectively channel their intended perspective so that they met their given words. In my opinion, this was not always successful; however, this experience has provided me with valuable insight into how I can improve in the future.
